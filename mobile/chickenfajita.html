Ingredients List

The ingredients for this dish are listed below. Chicken Fajitas are always a go to when you need a quick and tasty meal. They can be adapted to accomodate a variety of flavors and is a dish you always find yourself going back to.

  • Chicken Breast
  • Garlic Powder - 2 Tbsp
  • Onion Power - 1 Tbsp
  • Paprika - 1 Tbsp
  • Salt - 1 Tbsp
  • Pepper - 1 Tbsp
  • Cilantro
  • Halved Limes
  • Bell Pepper
  • Shredded Cheese
  • White Onion
  • Flour Tortillas

Cooking & Prep

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into thin strips.
  2. Season the chicken with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Ensure you pan is nice and hot with a bit of olive oil.
  4. Cook the chicken in the pan until your clear of all the pinkness.
  5. Chop one bell pepper into thin strips and your onion into small diced pieces.
  6. In a seperate heated pan with oil, throw in your peppers & onions cooking for 5-10 min.
  7. In a pan take your flour tortillas and then place the cooked chicken, along with the peppers and onions into them.
  8. Put your shredded cheese in there as well along with chopped cilantro
  9. Place the stuffed tortilla in a heated pan with oil and cook until the cheese melts and the sides of the tortilla are crispy
  10. Take your lemon halves and squeeze them over the tortillas and enjoy.
