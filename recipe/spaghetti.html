Ingredients List

The ingredients for this pasta dish are listed below. They are very simple ingredients that should be readily available at your local market or you may already have them in your home! Feel free to add any other seasonings that you like. This is just what i use for when i make my dish.

  • Hamburger Meat -1lb
  • Pasta Sauce - 1 Jar
  • Noodles - 1 Pack
  • Diced Baby Tomatoes - 1 pack
  • Garlic Powder - 2 Tbsp
  • Paprika - 1 Tbsp
  • Salt - 1 Tbsp
  • Pepper - 1 Tbsp
  • Onion Powder - 1 Tbsp
  • Italian Seasoning - 1 Tbsp
  • Butter - 1 Tbsp

Cooking & Prep

  1. Making spaghetti is all in the preperation and letting your flavors build. You want to season you meat with all the seasonings listed above.
  2. Next you will need to get your pan above medium heat and add some olive oil.
  3. Take your pot and add water, heating up to boil. Add in some salt & olive oil to the water as well.
  4. Drop that seasoned meat into the pan and cook until brown.
  5. Once the meat is cooked through, add the pasta sauce to the meat and cover with a lid to let simmer on low for about 15 min.
  6. Every few minutes you want to stir the meaty sauce and I like to add in some additional Italian seasoning to build up the flavor.
  7. Add in the small diced tomatoes and cover again. Once the noodles are cooked, drain the water and slowly add them to the pasta sauce.
  8. Finally, add in the butter to the spaghetti and stir. Let rest for a few mintues before you dig in and enjoy!
